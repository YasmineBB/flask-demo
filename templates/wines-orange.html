Orange Wines

Tryo, 2020

Alsace, France

Medium bodied, dry and acidic, this orange wine packs a citrus punch that remains light and fresh on the palate.

Tasting Notes: Citrus, blood orange and fresh greens.

Pairing Suggestions: Pork, Rich Fish, Asian food

Type: Orange | Vintage: 2020 | Sulfur: No Added | Varietal: Riesling, Sylvaner, Pinot Gris | Alcohol: 13.5% | Production: Biodynamic | Style: Complex

Partida Creus CX, 2020

Penedes, Spain

This is a sparkling wine but it has a surprising pepper nose to it that makes it far more complex than what you'd expect! Low ABV and unusual grape.

Tasting Notes:A funky and complex explosion of flavor! Pineapple, citrus, stone fruit, and bitter tannin on the palate.

Pairing Suggestions:Appetizers, Picnic Food, Summer Salads

Type: Orange | Vintage: 2020 | Sulfites: No Added | Alcohol: 10.5% | Varietal: Cartoixa Vermell | Production: Organic

Roditis Amphora, 2019

Achaia, Greece

Vibrant bronze in color, intense minerality, strong acidity with aromas of honeycomb and orange peel.

Tasting Notes: Citrus, tree fruit, and yeast notes!

Pairing Suggestions: Shellfish, Crab, Lobster

Type: Orange | Vintage: 2019 | Sulfur: 26 mg/L | Varietal: Roditis | Alcohol: 11% | Production: Organic, Biodynamic | Style: Nerdy
